# Srinivasa Ramanujan

## Key Figures

- **G.H. Hardy** — brought Ramanujan to Cambridge; *Twelve Lectures*; champion and collaborator
- **George Andrews** — discovered the Lost Notebook; *Theory of Partitions*
- **Bruce Berndt** — annotated all five notebooks; foremost Ramanujan scholar
- **Krishnaswami Alladi** — Ramanujan Journal; *Ramanujan's Place in the World of Mathematics*
- **K. Srinivasa Rao** — *Life and Work*; *The Role of Mentors*
- **Robert Kanigel** — *The Man Who Knew Infinity*; definitive popular biography
- **Ken Ono** — modern continuation; *My Search for Ramanujan*; *The Last Words of a Genius*
